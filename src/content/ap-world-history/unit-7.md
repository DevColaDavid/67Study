# Unit 7: Global Conflict (1900–Present)

## 🧠 Core Idea

The 20th century opened with massive internal and external pressures that toppled empires — Ottoman, Russian, Qing Chinese — and replaced them with new states. Two catastrophic **World Wars**, driven by nationalism, imperialism, economic collapse, and the rise of fascist/totalitarian regimes, reshaped the globe. New military technology made both wars far deadlier. The era also witnessed some of history's worst **mass atrocities**: the Armenian Genocide and the Holocaust.

---

## ⚡ Key Takeaways

- Three major empires collapsed: **Ottoman, Russian (→ Soviet), and Qing Chinese**.
- WWI causes = **MAIN**: Militarism, Alliances, Imperialism, Nationalism. Spark = assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand.
- WWI introduced **total war** and modern weapons → trench warfare stalemate.
- The **Great Depression** spread globally because European nations depended on US loans.
- WWI's unresolved tensions directly caused WWII.
- WWII's causes: **Treaty of Versailles humiliation**, **imperial aspirations**, **Great Depression**, and **rise of fascist/totalitarian regimes**.
- Both wars used **propaganda** to mobilize civilian populations.
- The **Holocaust** killed 6 million Jews and 5 million others. The **Armenian Genocide** killed 600,000–1.5 million Armenians.
- Atomic bombs on **Hiroshima and Nagasaki** ended the Pacific War and changed warfare forever.

## 📑 Section Breakdown

### 🏛️ A Major Shift in State Power (Early 20th Century)

#### Ottoman Empire
- **Young Turks** overthrew Sultan in **1908**: secularized schools/laws; started elections; made Turkish official language → alienated minorities → weakened empire.
- Ottoman loss in **WWI** → empire ended.

#### Russia → Soviet Union
- **Russian Revolution of 1905**: Growing middle and working class demanded voice → brutally suppressed.
- Tsar Nicholas II offered limited reforms; little changed.
- WWI hardships → **Russian Revolution of 1917**.
- **Vladimir Lenin** + Bolsheviks took power → **Soviet Union**, world's **first Communist State**.

#### Qing China
- Revolutionary movement led by **Sun Yat-Sen** → Qing emperor stepped down → ended **2,000 years of imperial rule**.
- Civil war → **Mao Zedong** and Communist Party took power by mid-20th century.

#### Mexico
- **Mexican Revolution**: Ousted dictator **Porfirio Diaz**. Madero assassinated. Pancho Villa and Emiliano Zapata led peasant armies. By **1917**, new constitution addressed grievances.

### 💥 Causes of World War I (1914–1918)

#### MAIN Causes ⭐

| Cause | Explanation |
|---|---|
| **Militarism** | Massive buildup of armies and navies, especially Britain vs. Germany |
| **Alliances** | Triple Alliance (Germany, Italy, Austria-Hungary) vs. Triple Entente (Russia, France, Britain) |
| **Imperialism** | Bitter rivalries from competing for lands in Africa and Asia |
| **Nationalism** | Pride in one's state defined against enemies → conflicts met with force |

**The Spark**: **Gavrilo Princip** (Serbian nationalist) assassinated **Archduke Franz Ferdinand** → triggered alliance system → WWI began.

---

### ⚔️ How WWI Was Fought

#### Total War
- **Definition**: Entire population mobilized — even civilians become legitimate targets.
- WWI was the **first total war** in history.

#### New Military Technology
- **Machine guns**, **chemical gas**, **tanks** → trench warfare stalemate; enormous casualties.

#### Propaganda
- **Definition**: Highly biased government information to create strong emotional responses.
- Demonized the enemy; intensified nationalism to justify sacrifices.

#### Outcome
- US joined **1917**; Britain and France won.
- **Treaty of Versailles (1918)**: Germany severely punished — full war blame ("war guilt clause"), stripped of military, enormous reparations.

---

### 📉 The Great Depression and Government Responses

**Why global**: European nations relied on US loans to recover from WWI. US stock market crash (1929) → loans stopped → global collapse.

| Country | Response |
|---|---|
| **United States** | **New Deal** (FDR): Infrastructure projects; government programs for retired, elderly, children |
| **Soviet Union** | Lenin's New Economic Plan → then Stalin's **Five Year Plans** + collectivization of agriculture |

> [!warning] Holodomor
> Ukraine = largest grain producer. Stalin's collectivization → catastrophic famine → genocide called **Holodomor** ("death by hunger").

---

### 🌏 Unresolved Tensions After WWI

- Colonial troops fought in WWI but **not granted independence**. Colonial holdings maintained + expanded via **League of Nations mandate system** = disguised imperialism.
- **Japan**: Invaded **Manchuria** (1931); League condemned them → Japan **left the League**; continued expansion under "Greater East Asia Co-Prosperity Sphere."
- **India**: Indian National Congress + **Mohandas Gandhi** grew the independence movement.

---

### 🔥 Causes of World War II

#### Four Main Causes ⭐
1. **Treaty of Versailles humiliation**: Italy denied territories; Germany humiliated by reparations + "war guilt clause."
2. **Imperial aspirations**: Japan's Manchuria invasion; Italy's Ethiopia invasion; Germany reclaiming territory under **Adolf Hitler** and the **Nazi Party**.
3. **Great Depression**: Desperate populations receptive to extreme politics.
4. **Rise of fascist/totalitarian regimes**: Stalin (USSR), Mussolini (Italy, first fascist state), Hitler (Germany), fascist Japan.

> [!note] Key Definitions
> - **Fascism**: Far-right extreme nationalism + authoritarian leadership + militarism.
> - **Totalitarianism**: Government controls ALL aspects of citizens' lives.

**Hitler's steps**: Canceled reparations; remilitarized Germany; annexed territory across Europe. **1939**: Invaded **Poland** → Britain and France declared war → WWII officially begins.

---

### ⚙️ How WWII Was Fought

- Both **total wars**. Both fought between alliances (**Axis**: Germany, Italy, Japan; **Allied**: Britain, France, USSR, + US after Pearl Harbor). Both used **propaganda**.
- **US**: Japanese immigrants placed in **internment camps** after Pearl Harbor.
- **Germany**: Nuremberg Laws stripped Jewish rights; Jews and others forced into **ghettos and concentration camps**.
- **New tech**: **Firebombing** of cities (Dresden, Tokyo) → hundreds of thousands of civilian deaths.
- **Atomic bombs** (Hiroshima and Nagasaki) → killed hundreds of thousands → ended Pacific War.

---

### ☠️ Mass Atrocities

#### Armenian Genocide
- Turkish nationalism under **Young Turks** → suspicion of Armenian Christians as potential Russian allies in WWI.
- **Ottoman authorities**: Mass extermination + forced relocation with **death marches**.
- **Deaths**: **600,000–1.5 million Armenians** killed.

#### The Holocaust
- **Nazi ideology**: A racially pure German state.
- **Hitler's "Final Solution"**: Industrial-scale extermination in **concentration camps**.
- **6 million Jews + 5 million others** killed.
- **Causes connect to**: Casta System (Unit 4) and Social Darwinism (Unit 6).
