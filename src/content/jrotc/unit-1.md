## Cadet Creed

> I am an Army Junior ROTC Cadet. I will always conduct myself to bring credit to my family, country, school and the Corps of Cadets. I am loyal and patriotic. I am the future of the United States of America. I do not lie, cheat, or steal and will always be accountable for my actions and deeds. I will always practice good citizenship and patriotism. I will work hard to improve my mind and strengthen my body. I will seek the mantle of leadership and stand prepared to uphold the Constitution and the American way of life. May God grant me the strength to always live by this creed.

> [!tip] Memorization Tip
> Break into 3 parts: **Who I am → What I won't do → What I will do**
> Don't forget "family, country, **school** and the Corps" — school is often left out!

## U.S. Army Ranks

### Enlisted Ranks (E-1 to E-9)

| Pay Grade | Rank | Abbr. | Insignia |
|---|---|---|---|
| **E-1** | Private | PV1 | No insignia |
| **E-2** | Private | PV2 | 1 chevron |
| **E-3** | Private First Class | PFC | 1 chevron + 1 rocker |
| **E-4** | Specialist / Corporal | SPC / CPL | Eagle device (SPC) or 2 chevrons (CPL) |
| **E-5** | Sergeant | SGT | 3 chevrons |
| **E-6** | Staff Sergeant | SSG | 3 chevrons + 1 rocker |
| **E-7** | Sergeant First Class | SFC | 3 chevrons + 2 rockers |
| **E-8** | Master Sergeant / First Sergeant | MSG / 1SG | 3 chevrons + 3 rockers / + diamond |
| **E-9** | Sergeant Major / CSM / SMA | SGM / CSM / SMA | 3 chevrons + 3 rockers + star or wreath |

> [!note] E-4 Split
> **SPC** = technical role, no leadership authority — wears eagle device
> **CPL** = same pay grade but is an NCO with leadership authority — wears 2 chevrons

> [!note] E-8 & E-9 Split
> **MSG** = staff role | **1SG** = leads a company, diamond in insignia ("First Shirt")
> **SGM** = staff | **CSM** = senior NCO of a command (wreath) | **SMA** = top enlisted in the Army

### Officer Ranks (O-1 to O-10)

| Pay Grade | Rank | Abbr. | Insignia |
|---|---|---|---|
| **O-1** | Second Lieutenant | 2LT | 1 **gold** bar |
| **O-2** | First Lieutenant | 1LT | 1 **silver** bar |
| **O-3** | Captain | CPT | 2 silver bars |
| **O-4** | Major | MAJ | **Gold** oak leaf |
| **O-5** | Lieutenant Colonel | LTC | **Silver** oak leaf |
| **O-6** | Colonel | COL | Silver eagle |
| **O-7** | Brigadier General | BG | 1 star |
| **O-8** | Major General | MG | 2 stars |
| **O-9** | Lieutenant General | LTG | 3 stars |
| **O-10** | General | GEN | 4 stars |
| **Special** | General of the Army | GA | 5 stars (wartime only) |

> [!note] Gold vs Silver — The Irony Rule
> **2LT (lower) = Gold** | **1LT (higher) = Silver**
> **MAJ (lower) = Gold oak leaf** | **LTC (higher) = Silver oak leaf**

> [!tip] Memory Trick — Officers
> **Bars → Leaves → Eagle → Stars** = *"Big Leaders Earn Stars"*

## LDRSHIP — Army Values

| Letter | Value | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **L** | Loyalty | Bear true faith to the Constitution, Army, unit, and fellow Soldiers |
| **D** | Duty | Fulfill your obligations; do what is right |
| **R** | Respect | Treat people as they should be treated |
| **S** | Selfless Service | Put the welfare of others before your own |
| **H** | Honor | Live up to all Army values |
| **I** | Integrity | Do what's right legally and morally — even when no one is watching |
| **P** | Personal Courage | Face fear, danger, or adversity (physical or moral) |

> [!tip] **L-D-R-S-H-I-P = LEADERSHIP** — every cadet leads!
