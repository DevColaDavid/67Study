How To Study This Unit

This unit should be studied through three layers: conceptual meaning, symbolic methods, and AP-style communication. Don’t memorize formulas in isolation; connect each tool to the type of question where it is strongest.

Practice short sets by subtopic first, then mixed sets that force method selection. In FRQ-style responses, write one brief sentence that interprets your result in context.

Series test workflow: nth-term check first, then structure-based test (geometric/p-series), then comparison/ratio/alternating, and endpoint checks for power series.
